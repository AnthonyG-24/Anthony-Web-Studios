Common questions about my web development process and how we'll work together

Simple 3-step process:

  1. You purchase your domain - I'll recommend Porkbun and guide you through the process if needed
  2. Share temporary access - Give me your login details so I can configure everything
  3. I handle the technical setup - DNS, nameservers, and connection to your website

You own the domain completely, I just handle the technical connection to make your website live!

Split payments for your protection:

  1. 50% deposit - Secure your project and I begin work immediately
  2. I build your website - Regular updates and communication throughout
  3. 50% final payment - Due when your website is complete and ready to launch

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. You'll receive email receipts for everything.

Here's what happens next:

  1. I review your project - Usually within 24 hours of your quote request
  2. We discuss your needs - I'll email you with questions and project timeline
  3. You pay the deposit - I'll send you a secure payment link to get started
  4. Project begins - Regular updates and communication throughout development

No pressure, no surprises - just clear communication every step of the way.
