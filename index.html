Terms & Policies

How Payments Work

Two payments total: You pay 50% upfront to start your project, then 50% when your website is complete and ready to launch. All payments are processed securely through Stripe, and you'll get email receipts for everything.

Package Pricing: Starter Package is $800 total ($400 + $400). Professional Package is $1,200 total ($600 + $600). Price stays the same whether you use all available pages or not.

Refund Policy (Simple & Fair)

Deposit (first 50%): Once I start working on your project, this deposit cannot be refunded. This covers my time and effort invested in your website.

Final payment (last 50%): If I don't deliver what we agreed on in your package, you can request a full refund within 7 days of completion. For example, if you paid for 4 pages but only got 2, or if a promised contact form is missing.

Project Timeline & Your Role

How long it takes: Starter Package (3-4 pages) = 1-2 weeks. Professional Package (up to 8 pages) = 2-3 weeks. Timeline starts when I receive your deposit.

What I need from you: Text content, images, your logo, and any specific requirements. I'll ask for these within 3 business days of starting. If you take longer to provide materials, it may extend the timeline.

Feedback windows: I'll show you progress and ask for feedback. You have 5 business days to respond, or I'll continue with what's been approved.

What's Included vs What Costs Extra

Your free support period: Starter Package gets 2 weeks free support. Professional Package gets 1 month free support. This starts after your website launches.

Always free (even after support period): Changing text, swapping images, fixing bugs, basic technical help.

Costs extra: Adding new pages ($125 each), custom features like galleries or special contact forms ($150+), major design changes.

Changes & Additions

During development: Small tweaks are included. Major changes (like adding extra pages or completely new features) will require additional payment and may extend your timeline.

After approval: Once you approve a design or section, changes to that approved work may incur additional fees.

Website Ownership & Access

You own your website: After final payment, your website is 100% yours with no ongoing fees to me. You have full control to update content, manage hosting, and make changes.

Domain ownership: You purchase and own your domain (like yoursite.com). I just help connect it to your website.

Protection & Responsibilities

My responsibility: Deliver a professional website that matches your package specifications and works properly.

Your responsibility: Provide content and feedback on time. Ensure any content you give me (text, images) is legal to use and doesn't violate copyrights.

Limitations: I'm not responsible for issues beyond my control (like your hosting going down). My total liability is limited to what you paid for the project.

Cancellation Policy

If you cancel: You can cancel anytime, but the deposit is non-refundable once work begins. You'll receive any completed work.

If I cancel: In the rare case I need to cancel, you'll get a full refund and any work completed for free.
